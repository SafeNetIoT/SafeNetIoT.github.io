Lab

PUBLISH ME: The UCL EEE SafeNetIoT lab focuses on the security and privacy of Internet of Things (IoT) devices. We investigate potential threats to IoT devices in various settings, including homes, hospitals, and industrial environments. The lab develops and evaluates security measures to protect these devices, collaborating with policymakers to create regulations, and working with industry to implement secure solutions. Our research aims to make IoT technology safer and more private for everyone.
